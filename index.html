Introduction to Mental Health

Mental health includes our emotional, psychological, and social well-being. It affects how we think, feel, and act, especially in coping with stress and making life choices.

Common Mental Health Disorders

  • Anxiety Disorders
  • Depression
  • Post-Traumatic Stress Disorder (PTSD)
  • Eating Disorders
  • Substance Use Disorders

Solutions for Mental Wellness

Here are some effective strategies to maintain and improve mental health:

  • Meditation and Mindfulness Apps
  • Therapy and Counseling (Online and In-Person)
  • Regular Physical Exercise and Proper Diet
  • Practicing Gratitude and Journaling
